# Initial Concept
Build a high-performance, visually stunning portfolio that scrolls horizontally. The user explores the developer's journey by scrolling down (which translates to horizontal movement). The vibe is "Swiss Design meets Cyber-Industrial".

# Product Guide: Protik - Cinematic Horizontal Portfolio

## 1. Vision
**"Protik"** is a desktop-first, cinematic portfolio website designed to captivate high-end freelance clients. It merges the precision of Swiss Design with the raw edge of Cyber-Industrial aesthetics, creating an immersive digital experience that showcases technical mastery and creative vision. The primary goal is to demonstrate "Awwwards-winning" quality, positioning the developer as a premium partner for ambitious digital products.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **High-End Freelance Clients:** Founders, creative directors, and businesses seeking bespoke, high-performance web experiences.
*   **Recruiters & Tech Leads:** Individuals scouting for top-tier talent with a mastery of modern frontend technologies.
*   **Design & Dev Community:** Peers looking for inspiration, establishing authority within the creative development space.

## 3. Core Value Proposition
*   **Immersive Storytelling:** Transforms the standard vertical scroll into a horizontal journey, mimicking a film strip or gallery.
*   **Technical Excellence:** Showcases deep expertise in React, animation physics, and performance optimization through the medium of the site itself.
*   **Premium Aesthetic:** The "Industrial Minimalist" design signals attention to detail and a modern design sensibility.

## 4. Key Features
*   **Horizontal Scroll System:** Frictionless, physics-based horizontal movement driven by vertical scroll interactions.
*   **Cinematic Hero Section:** A bold introduction with parallax typography and immersive visual textures.
*   **Interactive Case Studies:**
    *   **Bolt:** A specialized showcase layout.
    *   **Dukaan:** A dedicated section with hover-activated video previews.
    *   **Open Source:** A parallax grid highlighting community contributions.
*   **Live Tech Stack Marquee:** An infinite scrolling loop of technologies that interact with the cursor.
*   **Custom Cursor System:** Context-aware cursor that scales and reacts to hoverable elements, enhancing immersion.
*   **Desktop-First Experience:** A deliberate choice to focus on the desktop canvas, with a polite "Desktop Experience Only" guardrail for mobile devices.

## 5. Success Metrics
*   **Engagement:** Increased time on site and scroll depth (completion rate of the horizontal journey).
*   **Conversion:** High click-through rate on the "Contact" or "Start a Project" call-to-actions.
*   **Performance:** Consistently achieving 95+ Lighthouse scores for Performance and Best Practices.
